# Weekly Q2 Growth v0.1

## Purpose

This is not a Weekly skeleton.

This is not a Weekly section plan.

It only records the next observation after Q2 validation:

**if Q2 is alive, what second question does it naturally create?**

## Starting Point

Q2 has already shown proof of life:

`這週，我到底練到了什麼？`

It works across:

- build weeks
- recovery weeks
- disrupted weeks

It does not need more week types yet.

The next step is no longer to prove Q2 again.

The next step is to observe what Q2 itself asks for next.

## Current Observation

After reading the Q2 statement, the next natural question does not feel like:

- `證據是什麼？`
- `數據是多少？`
- `哪一堂課最強？`

The next question feels more like:

`什麼真正讓我學會了這件事？`

This is important.

Because it means Weekly does not move directly from learning to evidence.

It first moves from:

`Learning`

to

`Cause of Learning`

## Why This Matters

The second question is broader than:

`哪一堂課真正教會了我？`

Because what taught the week's learning may not be:

- one session
- one workout
- one standout day

It may instead be:

- one adjustment
- one cancellation
- one recovery decision
- one pattern across the whole week
- one choice to preserve rhythm instead of forcing completion

This makes the second question more faithful to the coach worldview already established in:

- Recovery Domain
- Load Build Domain
- Narrative Engine

## Example Movement

### Build Week

Q2:

`這週，你練到的是：開始承受更大的訓練刺激。`

Natural next question:

`什麼真正讓你開始承受這個刺激？`

### Recovery Week

Q2:

`這週，你練到的是：讓前面的刺激真正留下來。`

Natural next question:

`什麼真正讓你把刺激留下來，而不是只是把訓練做少？`

### Disrupted Week

Q2:

`這週，你練到的是：在狀態改變時，仍然守住整體節奏。`

Natural next question:

`什麼真正讓你守住了節奏？`

## What This Suggests

Weekly may be growing in a different order from Monthly.

Monthly grew from:

`Position`

to

`Evidence`

Weekly may instead grow from:

`Learning`

to

`Cause of Learning`

to

`Evidence`

This does not yet define the page.

It only suggests Weekly's reasoning order may be different.

## Boundary

This document does not yet decide:

- whether the second question becomes a section
- whether it is answered with one session, many sessions, or a weekly pattern
- whether evidence comes immediately after it
- what the final Weekly reading order should be

It only preserves the current discovery:

**Q2 seems to naturally create a second question about what truly caused the week's learning.**

## Next Step

Do not design Weekly yet.

Test whether this second question also feels true across real weeks before letting it define the page.
