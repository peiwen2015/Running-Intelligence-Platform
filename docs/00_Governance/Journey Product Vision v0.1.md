# Journey Product Vision

## Purpose

The platform should accompany the runner through a continuous training journey, not simply organize individual activities.

Its purpose is not to report one run at a time.

Its purpose is to help the runner feel understood across time.

中文：

平台存在的目的，不是整理一次次跑步，而是陪伴跑者完成一整段訓練旅程。

## Position

Journey is not a module.

Journey is the product spine.

All major product surfaces are part of the same journey:

- Today
- Weekly
- Monthly
- Shoes
- Training
- Metadata

These are not isolated pages.

They are different scales of the same coaching relationship.

## Why Journey Matters

Architecture Principles answer:

`How the system should be built`

Metadata Governance answers:

`How data should be governed`

Product Design Principles answer:

`How the product should speak and guide`

Journey Product Vision answers:

`Why the product exists in the first place`

Journey is not UI.

Journey is product philosophy.

## Principle 1: Growth Axis, Not Timeline

Journey should describe growth, not just time.

Not:

```text
2026-03
↓
2026-04
↓
2026-05
```

But:

```text
Begin
↓
Adapt
↓
Build
↓
Peak
↓
Recover
↓
Restart
```

The journey should reflect how the runner changes.

Not just when things happened.

## Principle 2: Milestones Matter More Than Dates

Each journey should include meaningful milestones.

Examples:

- First 10K
- First HM
- First Cruise Interval
- Boston 13 becomes primary shoe
- Sub-2 half marathon
- First stable heat-adaptation block

Milestones are more memorable than dates.

They are what make training feel real.

## Principle 3: Turning Points Should Be Remembered

Journey should preserve important turning points.

Examples:

- Started heat training
- Added HM pace work
- Shifted focus to recovery
- Changed primary shoe rotation
- First major breakthrough in consistency

Turning points give structure to a training story.

Without them, history becomes only a list of activities.

## Principle 4: Coaching Continuity

The product should behave like an ongoing coaching dialogue.

Not:

```text
Report
↓
Report
↓
Report
```

But:

```text
Coach
↓
Recommendation
↓
Runner
↓
Execution
↓
Coach
↓
New Recommendation
```

This is why memory matters.

The value of coaching is not a single suggestion.

It is the continuity between past advice, present execution, and next judgment.

## Principle 5: Reflection Over Raw Progress

Journey should not only show progress.

It should create reflection.

Examples:

- Recovery improved
- Threshold became more sustainable
- Heat became less disruptive
- Running form became more stable late in long runs

The most important outcome is not always a PB.

Sometimes the most important change is understanding what is becoming stronger.

## Principle 6: Identity Emerges Over Time

Journey should help the runner form a sense of identity.

For example:

```text
2025
Started running
↓
2026
Completed first HM
↓
2027
Sub-1:50
↓
2028
Able to coach others
```

Over time, the platform should help the runner see not only what they did, but who they are becoming.

## Product Implication

Journey should eventually become the primary framing surface of the product.

Not:

- Overview
- Weekly
- Monthly

But:

- Journey
- Today
- Weekly
- Monthly
- Shoes
- Training
- Metadata

Journey is the main narrative.

The other surfaces are supporting chapters.

## Design Implication

Journey should not be implemented as a simple timeline.

It should include:

- phases
- milestones
- turning points
- coaching continuity
- key sessions
- long-term identity signals

This makes the product feel like a coach who remembers, not a dashboard that refreshes.

## Relationship to Product Design Principles

If `Product Design Principles v1.0` defines:

`How each page should guide a decision`

then `Journey Product Vision v0.1` defines:

`How all pages together become one coherent coaching relationship`

## North Star

Every run becomes meaningful because it is part of a journey.

中文：

不是每一次跑步都很重要，而是每一次跑步，都讓整段旅程更完整。

## Closing Lines

Data explains the past. Journey gives it meaning.

中文：

資料解釋過去，旅程賦予它意義。

真正讓人記住的教練，不是因為某一次建議，而是因為他始終理解你一路走來的旅程。
